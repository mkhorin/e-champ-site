Card game Durak

The popular card game Durak supports up to six players. The goal of the game is to get rid of your cards. The first player to discard all cards is a winner. The last player left with the cards is a fool.

The following game options are available:

  • Siege durak
  • Transferable durak
  • Deck of 36 or 52 cards
  • Attack the loser first
  • Without trump
  • Lowest trump card beats trump ace
  • Max attacks at once
  • Max attacks before first discard
  • Number of cards dealt at once
  • Stop attack on picking up

A detailed description and rules of the Durak can be found in Wiki.
